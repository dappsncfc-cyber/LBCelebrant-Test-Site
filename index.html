Hello, I'm Lisa B Celebrant

Listening with heart. Creating with purpose.

"It's okay not to know where to begin."

Whether you're saying goodbye to someone you love, celebrating a wedding, welcoming a new child, renewing your vows, remembering a beloved pet or planning ahead for the future, you don't need to have all the answers or know exactly what you want to say.

That's what we'll discover together.

I never want our first meeting to feel like an interview. I want it to feel like a conversation over a cup of tea. There are no expectations and no pressure. You lead the way while I listen with curiosity, gently exploring the moments, memories and golden nuggets that make your story unlike anyone else's. From there, every carefully chosen word begins to come together, creating a ceremony that feels honest, personal and true to you.

It's often those unexpected moments that say the most. The story that makes everyone laugh. The memory that brings a tear. The little quirk, treasured tradition or everyday habit that captures the essence of a person, a relationship, a beloved pet or a family. Those are the moments that transform a ceremony from words on a page into something deeply personal and unforgettable.

I believe one of the greatest gifts we can offer one another is our presence. To truly listen without rushing. To understand without assumption. To create the time and space for stories to unfold naturally.

Every carefully chosen word, every pause and every detail is thoughtfully brought together to create a ceremony that truly reflects your story.

It would be an honour to take the time to tell your story beautifully.

Answering Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, in England and Wales, an independent celebrant cannot perform the legal marriage registration. Couples typically complete their legal registration (the "administrative script") at a local register office before or after their personalised, bespoke celebrant ceremony. However, your celebrant-led ceremony is where you share your real vows, exchange rings, and perform custom symbolic rituals (like handfasting) in front of all your family and friends.
To ensure smooth choreography, coordinate cue timings with venue suppliers, and allow standard time for stationery or Order of Service layout printing, all scripts must be approved and locked in your Secure Client Portal at least 14 days before the ceremony date.
As independent celebrants, we can incorporate a wide range of symbolic rituals, including traditional handfasting (binding of wrists with silk cords), ring-warming (where guests pass your rings to infuse them with good wishes), memory wish-trees, candle-lighting, or sand-blending rituals. All of these can be customised to match your family values.

Privacy Policy & Data Protection

UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018 Compliance Statement

At Lisa B Celebrant, we take your privacy and the security of your most personal stories seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, process, and protect your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a bespoke celebrancy service, we collect personal details (names, contact emails, phone numbers) and highly sensitive personal data (relationship stories, family histories, and personal reflections) submitted through our Secure Client Portal.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your standard personal data under the lawful basis of Contractual Necessity (to fulfill our celebrancy agreement). For sensitive personal stories, we process this data strictly under your Explicit Consent, which is granted when you engage our services and submit answers to your portal.

3. Cloud Security & Storage Boundaries

Your client portal is built upon Google Firebase cloud infrastructure. All data—including script drafts, uploaded readings, and photographs in the Document Vault—is encrypted both in transit (TLS) and at rest. We utilise strict Firebase Security Rules to ensure that your files can only be accessed by you (via authenticated magic link) and Lisa B Celebrant.

4. Data Retention Schedule

We do not hold your data indefinitely. Following the completion of your ceremony, your digital workspace, including all uploaded documents and script drafts, will be securely archived and permanently deleted from our active cloud servers after 12 months, allowing you time to download your final files.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you hold the right to Access (requesting a copy of your data), Rectification (correcting errors), and Erasure (the "right to be forgotten" before the standard retention period ends). To exercise these rights, please contact us directly via email.

If you are unsatisfied with our data handling, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK supervisory authority, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
