Answering Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, in England and Wales, an independent celebrant cannot perform the legal marriage registration. Couples typically complete their legal registration (the "administrative script") at a local register office before or after their personalised, bespoke celebrant ceremony. However, your celebrant-led ceremony is where you share your real vows, exchange rings, and perform custom symbolic rituals (like handfasting) in front of all your family and friends.
To ensure smooth choreography, coordinate cue timings with venue suppliers, and allow standard time for stationery or Order of Service layout printing, all scripts must be approved and locked in your Secure Client Portal at least 14 days before the ceremony date.
As independent celebrants, we can incorporate a wide range of symbolic rituals, including traditional handfasting (binding of wrists with silk cords), ring-warming (where guests pass your rings to infuse them with good wishes), memory wish-trees, candle-lighting, or sand-blending rituals. All of these can be customised to match your family values.

Privacy Policy

UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018 Compliance Statement

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Lisa B Celebrant. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy statement will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website, use our Secure Client Portal, or fill out our enquiry forms, and tell you about your privacy rights under UK law.

2. The Data Controller

Lisa B Celebrant is the data controller and is responsible for your personal data. If you have any questions regarding this policy or our data practices, please contact us at: privacy@lb-celebrant.co.uk.

3. Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: Includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier, and marital status.
  • Contact Data: Includes email address, telephone numbers, and billing addresses.
  • Ceremony Data: Planning timelines, ceremony dates, locations, story prompt details, guideparent names, and uploaded planning document vaults (such as PDF files, schedules, and photos).
  • Technical Data: Includes internet protocol (IP) address, login session states, browser type, and operating system.

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you (such as planning your bespoke milestone ceremony).
  • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests (or those of a third party) and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
  • Where we have your explicit consent to process sensitive personal data (such as information contained within your ceremony story questionnaires).

5. Storage and Security of Data

All client questionnaire data, planning files, and comments are stored inside our secure, encrypted **Google Firebase** database environment using regional EU storage boundaries. We enforce strict Firebase security and row-level access rules, ensuring your files and details are accessible strictly to you and Lisa B Celebrant.

6. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under UK data protection laws (UK GDPR) in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, or transfer of your personal data, and the right to withdraw consent at any time.

You also have the right to make a complaint at any time to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK regulator for data protection issues (www.ico.org.uk).
